Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 23, 2026

No Data Collected

Tree Line Clearance (Strike Calc) does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No account, login, or registration is required to use this app.

What the App Does

Tree Line Clearance is a field calculation tool for utility vegetation management professionals. It estimates tree strike risk and clearance margins relative to power conductors. All inputs you enter and results generated remain on your device only.

Device Permissions

The app may request access to device orientation sensors for the clinometer feature. This sensor data is used only for on-screen display calculations and is never stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Third Party Services

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages (github.io). No analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services of any kind are used. No third party has access to any data you enter into the app.

Important Disclaimer

All calculations produced by this app are estimates intended to assist professional judgment only. Results are not for final compliance determinations or engineering decisions. Users are responsible for verifying results against applicable standards.
